How to play

  • Click and select four cards.
  • Whether the card flips normally or upside down determines if you gain or lose luck.
  • The luck bar oscillates to determine the amount of luck you gain or lose with each card.
  • The luck of your fortune is determined by the total amount of luck you end with.
  • Pick wisely... and find out your fortune!
